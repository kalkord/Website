About The Developer

Crafting Digital Tools for Faith and Community

As a passionate developer, my mission is to create applications that are not only functional but also enrich people's lives. The Amharic Bible Application is a project born from a desire to make the scriptures more accessible and engaging for the Amharic-speaking community worldwide.

With a strong focus on user experience and performance, I specialize in building native mobile apps that are fast, reliable, and easy to use. Let's connect and build something great together.

App Features

Daily Verses & Reading Plans

Start your day with inspiring Amharic Bible verses and follow one-year reading plans including chronological and canonical options.

Advanced Search

Find any verse instantly with multi-word query support, translation switching, and offline search capabilities.

Study Tools & Notes

Highlight verses with different colors, take personal notes with integrated Amharic keyboard, and create verse wallpapers.

Cross-References & Lexicon

Explore enhanced cross-references and Strong's lexicon for deeper Bible study and theological understanding.

Share God's Word

Share verses via SMS, Facebook, WhatsApp, Messenger, and more. Create beautiful verse images for inspiration.

100% Offline Access

All Old and New Testament books available offline. No internet required for reading, searching, or studying.
